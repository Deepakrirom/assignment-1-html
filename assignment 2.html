Chicken Soup

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Water
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Fresh herbs
  • Option(noodles, potatoes, corn)

Preparation:

  1. Chop onion, garlic, carrots and wash chicken
  2. In a large pot, heat oil or butter
  3. Add veggies & chicken
  4. Add liquid
  5. Simmer the soup
  6. Shred the chicken
  7. Final Touches
